how i became a designer

For the past few years, I worked as a dietitian. While I appreciated the experience and the profession, I quickly realized that it wasn't the job for me. I wanted to create, to build, to design - things I have always loved to do. After work, I began learning how to use the design software and started to create more and more things on my own. I was desperate for a change in my career and I knew that graphic design was what I wanted to do. Determined and enthusiastic, I decided to make the change. This fall I started the graduate graphic design program at Boston University. During my time here, I have already gained many valuable skills that have made me a stronger designer. I am excited to continue to expand my knowledge and develop my skills over the next year and a half in the program.
