⟶ project one

poster: reliving a first experience

the project

This poster was created in my graduate studio class. The assignment was to recall a "first" experience (first time riding the train, first kiss, etc.) and recreate that memory in some way. This was my end result.

the process

  • 01 ⟶ writing
  • recalling the experience, remembering all of the smells, sounds, tastes and recreating the memories through a descriptive story
  • 02 ⟶ sketching
  • brainstorming how to represent the experience, sketching and sketching numerous iterations
  • 03 ⟶ pasta
  • five boxes of pasta later, discovering the best way to cook pasta when using it as a typeface

project two ⟵

editorial: discovering a designer's work

the project

For this assignment, we had to create an editorial about one of our peers.

the process

I started by gettining to know my "client" a little better by conducting interviews. I found out that she was a wonderful photographer and designer from China who was inspired by Japanese designers. I used her beautiful photographs throughout the book and added color, along with line drawings to tell her story. I then bound the book using a Japanese stab binding technique.

⟶ project three

website: the introverted girl on the train

the project

For this assignment, we had to create a storytelling website based on something that we tracked or collected.

the process

The focus of my site was my commute to and from school. For one week, I kept track of the total duration, the number of people near me on the train and any other interesting observations I noticed along the way.
